pub struct Texture3dMipmap<'t>(_, _);

Represents a single mipmap level of a Texture3d.

Can be obtained by calling Texture3d::mipmap(), Texture3d::main_level(), Texture3dLayer::mipmap() or Texture3dLayer::main_level().
