pub struct IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArrayMipmap<'t>(_, _);

Represents a single mipmap level of a IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArray.

Can be obtained by calling IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArray::mipmap(), IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArray::main_level(), IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArrayLayer::mipmap() or IntegralTexture2dMultisampleArrayLayer::main_level().
